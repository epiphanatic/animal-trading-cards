Dolphin

Research has suggested that dolphins possess self-awareness.

  • Infraorder: Cetacea
  • Average Length: 5.6 meters
  • Top Speed: 55 km/h
  • Average Lifespan: 44.5 years

Although dolphins are widespread, most species prefer the warmer waters of the tropic zones. Dolphins feed largely on fish and squid, but a few, like the killer whale, feed on large mammals, like seals. Dolphins produce a variety of vocalizations, usually in the form of clicks and whistles.
